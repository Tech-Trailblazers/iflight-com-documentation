Please follow this guide to re-flash the custom *.hex file for displaying the Betaflight OSD Warnings on your DJI Goggle:

1. Locate the *.hex file and download to your computer
2. Click on "Update Firmware" in the Betaflight Configurator
3. Make sure the target IFLIGHT_F722_TWING is selected
4. Click on "Load Firmware (Local)" and select the downloaded *.hex file.
5. Click on "Flash Firmware" and wait for it to finish
6. Click on "Connect" or reconnect your FC if not loaded automatically
7. Click on "Apply Custom defaults" to apply all the Flight Control related information
8. Apply our factory default diff (Can be found on our Google Drive)
