iFlight Chimera7 Pro HD (DJI Version) XING TUNE // Also for heavy batteries!

FC: iFlight Blitz F722 // BMI270 Gyro
ESC: iFlight Blitz E55 4in1 ESC

BLheli Settings Test Setup:
- Firmware 32.9
- Rampup Power: 40%
- Timing: 18 deg
- PWM Low: 24kHz (more authority at lower throttle or fast moves)
- PWM High: byRPM

Test Setup:
- HQ 7.5 inch props
- iFlight Fullsend LIPO 4.400mAh 6S (bottom mount, check for a good CG)
- iFlight Fullsend Li-Ion 4.000mAh 6S (top mount, check for good CG)

Radio Setup:
- TBS Tango2 Crossfire (firmware 6.17)
- Nano RX locked to 50hz (Don't use dynamic mode!)
- Betaflight 4.3.0 50hz preset applied

Tuning aspects:
- PID tuning with minimal overshoot, minimum Setpoint delay
- Smooth forward flights with bigger batteries
- Responsive performance without jitter
- Freestyle (faster moves) capability
- Priorized flight performance over flight time

Tuning tips for 48kHz PWM Speed (more flight time but worse flight performance due to low motor braking force)
- You can use PWM Low/High locked 48kHz/48kHz for a little longer flight times
- Add mode thrust_linear (24kHz 20%, 48kHz 30-50%)

WARNING:
- GPS rescue has been activated!
- Always make sure to do a test flight in confined area!
- Test your motor temperatures after applying a different tune!

Motor temperature:
- Big batteries can output high amps at high throttle
- Motors do get warm in aggressive flights
- Motors should not get too hot
- If your motors are getting too hot, reduce the Master slider by 0.1 in the Betaflight "PID Tuning" tab
